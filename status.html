How it stays fresh

  • Every 2 hours: weather/news/events are pulled by a Netlify Scheduled Function from National Weather Service, public RSS feeds, and the curated event list.
  • Every 5 minutes: traffic camera images refresh on the Traffic page when you have it open.
  • Daily at 1:05 AM: a remote agent in Anthropic's cloud probes every endpoint, scrapes city/school sites for new events, writes results here, and commits any changes.
