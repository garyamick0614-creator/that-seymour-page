Storm cleanup continues; cleanup-week meets a busy event calendar.

Updated April 29, 2026.

Storm recovery

April 27 storms left damage across Jackson County.

National Weather Service reporting confirmed multiple rounds of thunderstorms across central Indiana on April 27, with tornadoes, strong winds, hail, and flooding. Local coverage noted a tornado in Jackson County northwest of Seymour between Bobtown and Cortland. Cleanup is the focus; the storm landed during the city's annual Make Seymour Shine week (Apr 13-17), so curbside debris pickup capacity was already up.

What's happening around town

  • Crossroads Acoustic Festival — Apr 24-25 (recap on Seymour Main Street)
  • City Council meeting — Apr 27 (next: May 11 & 26, 7 PM, George Green Chambers)
  • Crossroads of Cultures — May 2
  • SCSC budget re-vote — May 1 (after Apr 17 614–924 rejection)
  • BurgerFest 2026 — Sep 19, downtown (36th year, Elexco main-stage sponsor)
