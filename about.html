What the shop does

Hardware & repair

We test before we touch. AIDA64 stress tests, HWiNFO sensor monitoring, multimeter rail checks. Thermal repaste, failed drive replacement with verified data migration, LCD backlight repair, board-level fault isolation. You get a diagnosis first — not a bill.

We do not perform laptop or phone screen replacements. The repair economics don't pay off at single-shop volume. We can refer you to trusted local screen specialists.

AI & automation

Custom workflow automation built in Python with LangChain and LangGraph. Email triage, document processing, invoice extraction, OSINT enrichment, customer-service routing. Local Ollama for privacy-sensitive work. Multi-provider integration with automatic failover.

Backups & cloud

Integration between Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, Dropbox, OneDrive, Backblaze B2, AWS S3, Wasabi. Automated schedules with verified restore tests, off-site replication, encryption at rest, version retention, tested DR playbooks. The 3-2-1 strategy implemented end to end.

Websites & software

Production websites for service businesses, contractors, and professionals. Next.js, static or dynamic, headless CMS integration, e-commerce, custom forms, Netlify or Vercel deployment with CI/CD. SEO-friendly structure, WCAG AA accessibility, performance budgets enforced.

Pentesting & security

Authorized security testing for businesses that want to know what their attack surface looks like. Network scoping, web-app testing, social-engineering simulation, written report with prioritized remediation. Defensive work too: SOC tuning, log review, incident response.

Training

One-on-one or small-group instruction for IT pros, students, and motivated hobbyists. Topics: Windows admin, networking fundamentals, cloud (Azure / AWS / GCP), scripting (PowerShell, Bash, Python), virtualization, certification prep (CompTIA A+, Network+, Security+, CCNA).

How TCG covers Seymour

Primary focus: Jackson, Bartholomew, Scott, Jennings, Brown counties (TCG's service area). Secondary: Columbus, Bloomington, Indianapolis metro. National-reach companies operating locally are in scope when they affect Indiana consumers.

IBE is funded by the parent brands (TCG26 / TCG Solutions). No sponsor relationships with covered businesses, no paid-to-review, no advertising. If a business becomes a TCG client, IBE recuses from covering them and discloses the relationship on any prior coverage.
